Statistics Learner || Statistics Lover

Hi! I am Sobrul Islam, a student of the Statistics Department at Jagannath University, Dhaka, Bangladesh. I am from Natore, Rajshahi. I love statistics and want to teach it in a simple and easy way so everyone can understand. My goal is to help students learn statistics without fear and make it interesting and practical.

My journey in statistics began with a curiosity about data and patterns. Now, I'm dedicated to sharing my knowledge through simple explanations, practical examples, and engaging content that makes statistics accessible to all.

Through Sobrul's Stat Talk, I aim to create a community where students can learn, ask questions, and develop confidence in statistical analysis.

Education

I started my primary education at 16th No. Brogaon Government High School, where I studied up to class five. After passing the final exam, I got admitted to Biash Batelian High School and completed both my JSC and SSC there. This was one of the happiest times of my life, full of good experiences and memories. Later, I studied at Singra Damdama Pilot High School and College, a well-known institution in that area, where I completed my HSC. Now, I am studying for a Bachelor of Science in Statistics at Jagannath University, Dhaka, with the aim of building a strong academic background and making a valuable contribution in the field of data and research.
